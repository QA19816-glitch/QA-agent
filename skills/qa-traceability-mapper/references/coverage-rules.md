# Coverage rules

## Mapping rules
- One requirement may map to many test points.
- One test point may map to many test cases.
- One defect may justify retest of multiple cases.
- Missing links should remain visible; do not invent them.

## Coverage quality prompts
- Is only the happy path covered?
- Are role differences covered?
- Are failure and recovery paths covered?
- Are integrations and notifications covered?
- Are historical defects regressed?
