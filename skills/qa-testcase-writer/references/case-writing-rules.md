# Case writing rules

## Strong case qualities
- One clear purpose per case
- Preconditions are explicit and reproducible
- Steps are short and ordered
- Expected results are visible and measurable
- Priority reflects business impact, not personal preference

## Priority shorthand
- P0: release-blocking core flow
- P1: major business path or high-risk validation
- P2: normal coverage
- P3: low-risk or cosmetic coverage

## Candidate automation signals
- Stable selectors or API contracts exist
- Test data can be prepared deterministically
- Case runs frequently in smoke/regression
- Assertion criteria are objective
